Earthkeeper

How to Use

UK Energy Clearing Tracker

Signing in

Enter your name, the group password, and pick your default clearing tool. If you work with Earthworks, tick the checkbox — that unlocks the EW1–5 options when you record a clearing.

Your preferences are remembered on this device. You can change your name, default tool, or EW status anytime from the Account menu in the top-right.

Coverage

Earthkeeper covers the whole UK — England, Wales, Scotland, Northern Ireland. Locations include hospitals, hospices, prisons, universities, schools and GP surgeries. Use the region dropdown to zoom into a specific area, or leave it on All regions to see everything.

Top bar

Three things live in the top header:

  • Stats tiles — context-sensitive to whichever tab is active (see below).
  • Protocol — opens the clearing protocol (the recommended sequence of structures to run on every location).
  • Account menu — shows your name, current default tool and Earthworks status. Expand it to change any of those, open these instructions, or sign out.

The stats tiles

  • Locations tab — total visible locations, how many are cleared, and the percentage done. Updates live as you toggle the place-type chips.
  • Groups tab — total schools + GPs across the UK, how many are cleared, percentage done, plus a fourth tile showing districts where both schools and GPs have been fully cleared.
  • Log tab — total locations everywhere, how many are cleared, and the percentage done. Flips between all group members’ clearings and just yours via the All / My Clearings toggle.

Locations tab

The main map. Dots show every location in the database across the UK. Cleared locations render as solid gold.

Use the place-type chips to show hospitals, hospices, unis, prisons, schools or GPs. The Show row lets you view everything, only cleared points, or filter by a specific tool. What are these? opens a key explaining the tool codes.

Tap any dot on the map or any item in the list to open its detail panel at the bottom of the sidebar. The home button (top-left of the map) resets the view to the whole UK.

Recording a clearing

Open a location’s detail panel. Your default tool is shown — tap Use a different tool to override it for this clearing. If you have Earthworks enabled, an optional EW level selector appears too.

Press Mark cleared to save. Your name, date, tool, and EW level (if used) are recorded and shared with the group straight away — other members see your update in real time.

To undo, open the same location and press Mark as not cleared.

Groups tab

Clear an entire Local Authority District at once rather than location-by-location. Useful when you’ve worked across a whole area. Covers all UK districts.

Select a district from the list to zoom in and see its schools (blue dots) and GP surgeries (green dots). The clearing panel opens inline at the bottom of the sidebar — pick which types to clear (Schools, GPs, or both), choose your tool, then press Mark cleared.

District glow shows progress: half-glow means one type has been cleared; full brightness means both schools and GPs are done.

Group clearings appear in the Log with a GROUP badge. Clicking a group entry in the Log jumps you back to that district.

Log tab

Full history of clearings, most recent first.

  • All Clearings — what the whole group has done.
  • My Clearings — only clearings recorded against the name on your account. Tracks what you personally have achieved.

The header stats and the map both follow the toggle: switching to My Clearings filters the cleared-dot layer to only your work.

Tap any log row to open that location’s detail panel without leaving the Log tab. Group entries jump you over to the Groups tab.

Find Me

The Find Me button (bottom-left of the map) uses your device’s location to zoom the map so you can see your position plus the nearest 15 hospitals, hospices, universities and prisons — roughly a district-scale view.

Clearings save automatically to the shared record. Every group member sees updates in real time — no need to refresh.
