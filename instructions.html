How to Use Earthkeeper

UK Energy Clearing Tracker — Guidance Notes

What is Earthkeeper?

Earthkeeper is a shared tracker for a group of UK energy healing practitioners. It maps locations across England — hospitals, hospices, prisons, universities, schools, and GP surgeries — and records which have been energetically cleared, by whom, and with which tool.

The goal is simple: see what has been done, coordinate who does what next, and build a shared record of the work over time.

Logging In

Enter your full name and the group password to access the tracker. Your name will be attached to any clearings you record, so others can see who did what in the log.

If you have forgotten the password, contact the group administrator.

The Three Tabs

Locations

This is the main view. The map shows coloured dots for every location in the database. Use the filter chips at the top of the panel to choose which types of place to display — hospitals, hospices, prisons, universities, schools, or GP surgeries.

To find a specific location, type its name or postcode into the search bar. Click any result to open its detail panel and see the map fly to it.

Tip: use the region dropdown to narrow down to a specific part of the country. This makes the map faster and the list easier to browse.

Recording a Clearing

Click on any location — either from the list or directly on the map — to open its detail panel. If it has not been cleared yet, you will see three tool buttons:

  • Omega — first level clearing
  • Jewel — second level clearing
  • MG — third level clearing

Select the tool you used, then press the gold Mark cleared button. Your name, the date, and the tool will be saved and shared with the whole group automatically.

If a clearing was recorded in error, open the location and press Mark as not cleared to remove it.

Groups

The Groups tab allows you to clear an entire Local Authority District at once, rather than recording each school or GP surgery individually. This is useful when a practitioner has worked energetically across a whole area.

Select a district from the list — or search for it — and the map will zoom in to show all the schools (blue dots) and GP surgeries (green dots) within it. The panel shows how many locations are in the district and how many have been individually cleared so far.

To record a group clearing, choose your tool and press Clear all Schools or Clear all GPs. The district will light up on the map. Clearing one group gives a half-glow; clearing both brings it to full brightness.

Group clearings appear in the Log tab alongside individual clearings, marked with a GROUP badge.

Log

The Log tab shows a full history of all clearings recorded by everyone in the group, most recent first. You can filter by date range using the From and To fields. Click any entry to jump to that location on the Locations tab.

Group clearings show in a different colour depending on whether schools or GPs were cleared, and are marked GROUP so they are easy to distinguish.

The Map

Dot colours tell you what type of location it is:

  • Red — Hospital
  • Teal — Hospice
  • Amber — Prison
  • Purple — University
  • Blue — School
  • Green — GP Surgery
  • Gold — Cleared (any type)

A cleared location turns gold. The colour of the ring around it shows which tool was used — purple for Omega, red-orange for Jewel, blue for MG.

Use the Find Me button to centre the map on your current location. Use Home to return to the full UK view.

Saving

Clearings are saved to a shared record automatically when you mark them. A small Saved notice will appear briefly at the bottom of the screen to confirm. All members of the group will see your clearing the next time they load the page.

The tracker stores all data in a shared repository. Nothing is ever deleted permanently — all changes are recorded with a timestamp and can be reviewed if needed.
