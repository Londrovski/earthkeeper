Earthkeeper

How to Use

UK Energy Clearing Tracker

Logging in

Enter your name, the group password, and select your default clearing tool. If you work with Earthworks, tick the checkbox — this unlocks the EW1–5 options when recording a clearing.

Your preferences are remembered, so you only need to do this once per device.

Coverage

Earthkeeper covers the whole of the UK — England, Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland. Locations include hospitals, hospices, prisons, universities, schools and GP surgeries. Use the region dropdown to zoom into a specific area, or leave it on All regions to see everything at once.

The header stats bar

The three numbers across the top of the page update depending on which tab you are on:

  • Locations tab — shows the total number of visible locations, how many are cleared, and the percentage done. These update live as you toggle the type filter chips.
  • Groups tab (overview) — shows districts cleared out of total, total individual locations cleared across all districts, and the percentage of districts done.
  • Groups tab (district selected) — switches to showing locations, cleared and percentage for that district only.
  • Log tab — shows two sets of stats separated by a divider: individual locations (hospitals, hospices, universities and prisons) cleared with a percentage, and then total locations including schools and GPs with a percentage.

Locations tab

The main map. Dots show all locations in the database across the whole UK. Cleared locations show as solid gold.

Use the filter chips at the top of the panel to show by location type or by clearing tool used. The What are these? key explains the tool codes. Use the region dropdown to narrow down to a specific area.

Click any location on the map or in the list to open its detail panel. The home button (top left of the map, below the zoom controls) resets the view back to the full UK.

Recording a clearing

Open a location's detail panel. Your default tool is shown — tap Use a different tool if you need to override it for this clearing. If you have Earthworks enabled, an optional EW level selector also appears.

Press Mark cleared to save. Your name, date, tool, and EW level (if used) are recorded and shared with the group immediately.

To undo, open the location and press Mark as not cleared.

Groups tab

Clear an entire Local Authority District at once rather than location by location. Useful when you have worked across a whole area. This tab covers all UK districts — England, Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland.

Select a district from the list to zoom in and see its schools (blue dots) and GP surgeries (green dots) on the map. Choose your tool and press Clear all Schools or Clear all GPs.

District glow indicates progress: a half-glow means one type has been cleared; full brightness means both schools and GPs are done.

To deselect a district and return to the overview, press the home button (top left of the map) or click on the sea.

Group clearings appear in the Log with a GROUP badge. Click any group entry in the Log to jump back to that district.

Log tab

Full history of all clearings, most recent first, from everyone in the group. Filter by date range if needed. Click any entry to jump to that location or district on the map.

The map on the Log tab shows all cleared locations across all types at once. The header shows two progress figures: individual locations (hospitals, hospices, universities and prisons), and then total locations including schools and GPs.

Clearings are saved to a shared record automatically. All group members see your changes the next time they load the page.
